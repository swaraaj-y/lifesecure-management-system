Life Cover Plan

Overview

Life Cover Plan provides complete financial protection to your family in case of unforeseen events. It ensures that your loved ones remain financially stable even in your absence. The plan offers guaranteed payout along with flexible policy terms.

Key Features

  • Guaranteed Sum Assured payout
  • Affordable premium starting from ₹500/month
  • Flexible policy term (10–30 years)
  • Option to increase coverage
  • Online policy management

Benefits

  • Complete financial security for family
  • Tax benefits under Section 80C & 10(10D)
  • Loan facility against policy
  • Accidental death benefit rider available
  • Critical illness rider available

Coverage Details

  • Coverage Range: ₹5 Lakhs to ₹25 Lakhs
  • Premium: ₹500/month onwards
  • Maturity Benefit: As per selected term
  • Death Benefit: Full Sum Assured payout

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum Age: 18 years
  • Maximum Age: 60 years
  • Minimum Policy Term: 10 years
  • Maximum Policy Term: 30 years
  • Indian Resident

Exclusions

  • Suicide within first 12 months
  • Fraud or misrepresentation of information
  • Death due to illegal activities

Why Choose This Plan?

This plan is ideal for individuals who want long-term protection at an affordable cost. It helps in future planning, child education funding, and loan protection. The policy ensures peace of mind with guaranteed benefits.
