Health Insurance Plan

Overview

Health Insurance Plan provides comprehensive medical coverage for hospitalization, surgery, and treatment expenses. It protects you and your family from unexpected medical costs and ensures quality healthcare without financial burden.

Key Features

  • Cashless treatment at network hospitals
  • Pre & post hospitalization coverage
  • Free annual health check-ups
  • No Claim Bonus benefit
  • Daycare procedures covered

Benefits

  • Coverage for hospitalization expenses
  • Ambulance charges covered
  • Room rent & ICU expenses included
  • Critical illness coverage option
  • Tax benefits under Section 80D

Coverage Details

  • Coverage Range: ₹3 Lakhs – ₹10 Lakhs
  • Premium: ₹800/month onwards
  • Family Floater option available
  • Maternity benefit add-on available

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum Age: 5 years
  • Maximum Age: 65 years
  • Medical test may be required
  • Indian Resident

Exclusions

  • Cosmetic or aesthetic treatments
  • Self-inflicted injuries
  • Pre-existing diseases (waiting period applicable)
  • Non-prescribed treatments
