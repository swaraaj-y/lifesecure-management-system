Term Insurance Plan

Overview

Term Insurance Plan provides high life coverage at very affordable premiums. It is designed to protect your family financially in case of your unfortunate demise during the policy term.

Key Features

  • High sum assured at low premium
  • Flexible policy tenure
  • Option to add riders
  • Easy online claim process
  • Premium payment options (Monthly / Yearly)

Benefits

  • Financial protection for dependents
  • Tax benefits under Section 80C & 10(10D)
  • Accidental death rider option
  • Critical illness rider available
  • Peace of mind with long-term security

Coverage Details

  • Coverage: ₹10 Lakhs and above
  • Premium: Affordable starting rates
  • Policy Term: 5–35 years
  • Death Benefit: Full Sum Assured payout

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum Age: 18 years
  • Maximum Age: 55 years
  • Medical examination may be required
  • Indian Resident

Exclusions

  • Suicide within first 12 months
  • Fraud or misrepresentation
  • Death due to criminal activities
