Senior Citizen Health Plan

Overview

Senior Citizen Health Plan is specially designed for individuals above 60 years of age. It provides financial support for hospitalization, medical treatments, and age-related illnesses. This plan ensures peace of mind and security during retirement years.

Key Features

  • Cashless hospitalization at network hospitals
  • Pre & post hospitalization coverage
  • Annual health check-ups
  • Lifelong renewability option
  • 24×7 customer support

Benefits

  • Coverage for age-related diseases
  • Room rent & ICU charges included
  • Daycare procedures covered
  • Ambulance expenses covered
  • Tax benefits under Section 80D

Coverage Details

  • Coverage: ₹2.5 Lakhs – ₹10 Lakhs
  • Premium: Flexible monthly & yearly options
  • Policy Term: 1 year (renewable)

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum Age: 60 years
  • Maximum Age: 80 years (entry age)
  • Medical check-up may be required
  • Indian Resident

Exclusions

  • Pre-existing diseases (waiting period applicable)
  • Cosmetic treatments
  • Self-inflicted injuries
  • Non-prescribed treatments
