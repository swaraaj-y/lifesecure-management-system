Accidental Insurance Plan

Overview

Accidental Insurance Plan provides financial protection in case of accidental death, permanent disability, or partial disability. It ensures that you and your family are financially secure against unexpected accidents.

Key Features

  • 24x7 worldwide accidental coverage
  • Permanent & partial disability benefits
  • Affordable premium rates
  • Easy and quick claim process
  • Optional hospital cash benefit rider

Benefits

  • Accidental death benefit payout
  • Compensation for permanent disability
  • Weekly benefit for temporary disability
  • Hospitalization expense support
  • Ambulance charges covered

Coverage Details

  • Coverage: ₹5 Lakhs – ₹20 Lakhs
  • Premium: Affordable monthly plans
  • Policy Term: 1–10 years (renewable)

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum Age: 18 years
  • Maximum Age: 65 years
  • Available for salaried & self-employed individuals

Exclusions

  • Self-inflicted injuries
  • Accidents under influence of alcohol/drugs
  • Participation in hazardous activities (unless covered)
  • War or nuclear risks
