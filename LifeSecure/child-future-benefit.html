Child Future Plan

Overview

Child Future Plan is designed to secure your child's education, higher studies, and important life milestones. It helps parents build a financial fund to support their child's dreams without stress.

Key Features

  • Long-term savings plan
  • Guaranteed maturity benefit
  • Premium waiver in case of parent’s death
  • Flexible premium payment options
  • Bonus benefits (if applicable)

Benefits

  • Financial support for higher education
  • Marriage fund planning
  • Protection benefit for parent
  • Tax benefits under Section 80C
  • Loan facility available

Coverage Details

  • Coverage: ₹4 Lakhs and above
  • Flexible policy term: 10–25 years
  • Premium: Affordable monthly options
  • Maturity payout at end of term

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum Child Age: 0 years
  • Maximum Child Age: 15 years
  • Parent Age: 18–55 years
  • Indian Resident

Exclusions

  • Fraud or false information
  • Policy lapse due to non-payment
